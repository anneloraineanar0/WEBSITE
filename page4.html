Information of the Song

  • Artist: Post Malone
  • Album: Stoney (Debut studio album)
  • Release Date: December 9, 2016
  • Single: October 17, 2017 (released after a live performance of the song went viral)
  • Genre: Hip-hop, R&B
  • Songwriters:The song was written by Post Malone (Austin Post), William Walsh, and the producer, Carlo "Illangelo" Montagnese.
  • Producer:Illangelo is the sole producer of the song. He is also credited with playing all instruments and programming.
  • Theme:The song's lyrics revolve around heartbreak, betrayal, and the emotional aftermath of a failed relationship.
  • Subject Matter: Post Malone sings about a girl who told him he wasn't "enough" and left him with a broken heart. He describes feeling "scarred" and trying to cope with the pain through drinking ("takin' these shots like it's Novocaine").

    • Description List

    Novocaine
    This is a brand name for a local anesthetic, which is a drug used to numb a specific area and block pain. In the song, Post Malone sings, "And now I'm takin' these shots like it's Novocaine." He's not literally using the drug; he is using alcohol ("takin' these shots") as a way to numb the emotional pain of his heartbreak, likening the effect of the liquor to the numbing power of Novocaine.
